Recipe #1: Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Ground Beef 300g

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.

Step 2: In a pan, cook the ground beef until browned. Add your favorite tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.

Recipe #2: Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup

Step 1: Mix the flour and milk until smooth.

Step 2: Heat a non-stick pan and pour the batter to form small circles. Cook until golden on both sides.

Recipe #3: Caesar Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Romaine Lettuce 1 head
Croutons 1 cup

Step 1: Chop the romaine lettuce and place it in a bowl.

Step 2: Add croutons and Caesar dressing. Toss well.

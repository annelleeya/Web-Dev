Bali is the only Hindu-majority province in Indonesia, renowned for its arts and culture, from traditional dance to metalworking.

Come explore its breathtaking beaches, ancient temples, and vibrant markets.

  • Visit ancient temples like Tanah Lot.
  • Explore vibrant Ubud markets.
  • Try exciting water sports.
  • Hike through scenic mountains.

  2. Seminyak Beach
  3. Mount Batur
  4. Ubud Monkey Forest
  5. Sanur Village
